Analysis of Income in Maryland

In this project, I studied the income trends of all counties in Maryland from the year 2010 to 2019. There was an increase in income trends so, I used a linear model to identify if it has any affect on the poverty rates.

Tools used: Jupyter, Python, pandas, scikit-learn, matplotlib, numpy, statsmodel

Analysis of Diabetes Measures

In this project, I studied the different diagnostic measures that could be used to identify if an individual has diabetes. I trained a linear model with the different measures and tested the model to see how well it could predict if a person has diabetes.

Tools used: R, RMySQL
